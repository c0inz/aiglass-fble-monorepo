A race condition risk exists on concurrent edits because there is no pessimistic concurrency control or cross-engine consistency check.

Automation runs log to `ai_automation_run`, not `ai_event_stream`, and no inbox events are emitted.

- Error handling across engine calls in `routes/projects.js` (lines 1576, 1594) is best-effort: errors are logged but do not roll back prior engine changes, leaving potential for inconsistent state.
